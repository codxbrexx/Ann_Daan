Welcome to Anna Daan

Our Mission

At Anna Daan, we believe that no one should go hungry while good food goes to waste. Our mission is to create a sustainable and efficient food donation ecosystem that connects surplus food with those in need, fostering a sense of community and social responsibility.

How It Works

Food is Donated

Generous donations of surplus and fresh food from local businesses, farmers, and community members.

Food is Secured

Food is secured through our network of generous donors and partners, ensuring it reaches those in need.

Food is Picked Up

Our team ensures that every donation is picked up promptly and delivered safely to those who need it most.
